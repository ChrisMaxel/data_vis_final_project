Data to Research ================= We plan to research and build complex datasets for the **Underlying and previous conditions of hospitalized or diseased Coronavirus patients**. Questions ============== Topic ------
We are exploring the outcomes of COVID-19 ill or diseased patients and the underlying conditions they had. This may help identify key risks and how deadly each condition could be. They are relevant because this could help people better understand their risks of dying from COVID-19.


Audience ----------
People who are concerned, curious, or researching the state of the current COVID-19 pandemic.


Our Plan ------------------
We would like our visualizations to be **interactive** so our audience can be fully immersed into our visualizations.
We would also like **intuitive**, **honest**, and **unbiased** visualizations


Impact --------
We hope that these data visualizations will help people who are worried or interested in COVID-19 better understand the overall death rate and hospitalizations by displaying what patients have in common.
